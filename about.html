Arabian Nights Photo Booth

This playful, browser-based photo booth lets you capture a selfie in front of a green screen and instantly transport yourself into iconic Arabian Nights scenes. It runs entirely on your device using your camera and local images.

How it works

We perform simple chroma keying in a canvas to remove the green background, then composite your image on themed backdrops. Choose your preferred aspect ratio for portrait or landscape shots.

Privacy

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos are not uploaded to a server. You can download your final image directly.

Credits
