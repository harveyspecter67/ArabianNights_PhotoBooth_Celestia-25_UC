Privacy

Your Privacy Matters

Arabian Nights Photo Booth processes your photos locally in your browser using the camera and an HTML canvas. We do not upload your images to any server.

Camera Access

The site requests permission to use your camera solely to capture a frame. You can revoke access anytime via your browser settings.

Image Processing

Chroma keying and compositing are performed on your device. When you download the final image, it is generated locally.

Storage

We do not persist or store your photos. Your session data is cleared when you refresh or close the page.

Third‑Party Content

If you choose remote backgrounds, exporting may be blocked by CORS. For full privacy and reliability, use the built‑in offline backgrounds.
